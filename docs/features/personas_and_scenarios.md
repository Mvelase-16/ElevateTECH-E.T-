# User Personas and Scenarios 

## Persona 1: Zolile Mtumela (Professor)

- **Age**: 43
 
- **Occupation**: University Lecturer (Data Structures)

- **Goal**: Quickly access and order food online due to a busy academic schedule.

## Persona 2: Nandi (Cafeteria Staff Member)
- **Age**: 35  
- **Occupation**: Cafeteria Worker  
- **Goal**: Manage food preparation efficiently and reduce workload pressure during peak hours  

### Scenario  
Nandi works in the university cafeteria and often struggles during lunch hours due to a sudden rush of students placing orders at the same time. This leads to stress and delays in service.  

With the Cafeteria Pre-Order App, Nandi can view all incoming orders in advance, organized by time slots. She prepares meals ahead of time based on scheduled pickups.  

This allows her to manage her workload better, reduce stress, and serve students more efficiently without being overwhelmed.

## Persona 3: Thando (Student Athlete)
- **Age**: 22  
- **Occupation**: Sports Science Student  
- **Goal**: Get healthy meals on time without interrupting training sessions  

### Scenario  
Thando has daily training sessions and limited time to get meals. Waiting in long cafeteria lines affects his training schedule and performance.  

Using the app, Thando selects a healthy meal option and schedules a pickup time right after his training session.  

He arrives at the cafeteria, collects his pre-prepared meal immediately, and continues his routine without delays.
