# User Personas and Scenarios 

## Persona 1: Zolile Mtumela (Professor)

- **Age**: 43
- **Occupation**: University Lecturer (Data Structures)
- **Goal**: Quickly access and order food online due to a busy academic schedule.

### Scenario

Prof. Mtumela has a full teaching schedule on Thursdays with only a 45-minute window between his 10am and 2pm lectures. Rather than walking to the cafeteria and risking a long queue, he opens the Campus Food Ordering App from his office. He filters the menu by "healthy options" and selects a grilled chicken wrap and bottled water from a nearby vendor. He pays using mobile payment and selects a pickup time of 12:30pm. At 12:25pm, he receives a notification that his order is ready. He walks to the pickup point, collects his meal, and is back at his desk within 10 minutes — leaving him enough time to eat and review his afternoon lecture notes.

## Persona 2: Nandi (Cafeteria Staff Member)
- **Age**: 35  
- **Occupation**: Cafeteria Worker  
- **Goal**: Manage food preparation efficiently and reduce workload pressure during peak hours  

### Scenario  
Nandi works in the university cafeteria and often struggles during lunch hours due to a sudden rush of students placing orders at the same time. This leads to stress and delays in service.  

With the Cafeteria Pre-Order App, Nandi can view all incoming orders in advance, organized by time slots. She prepares meals ahead of time based on scheduled pickups.  

This allows her to manage her workload better, reduce stress, and serve students more efficiently without being overwhelmed.

## Persona 3: Thando (Student)
- **Age**: 22  
- **Occupation**: Sports Science Student  
- **Goal**: Get healthy meals on time without interrupting training sessions  

### Scenario  
Thando has daily training sessions and limited time to get meals. Waiting in long cafeteria lines affects his training schedule and performance.  

Using the app, Thando selects a healthy meal option and schedules a pickup time right after his training session.  

He arrives at the cafeteria, collects his pre-prepared meal immediately, and continues his routine without delays.

## Persona 4: Sipho (Student)

- **Age**: 21
- **Occupation**: 3rd-year Computer Science student at WSU
- **Tech Comfort**: High — uses smartphone apps daily
- **Goal**: Order food quickly between lectures without wasting time standing in long queues at the campus cafeteria.
- **Pain Points**: Long queues during lunch breaks, limited time between classes, uncertainty about whether a stall is open or sold out.

### Scenario

Sipho has a 30-minute break between two back-to-back lectures.

He opens the Campus Food Ordering App, browses the available stalls, and filters by "ready in under 15 minutes." He selects a toasted sandwich and a juice from the Main Cafeteria, adds them to his cart, and pays using his campus card.

He receives a notification when his order is ready for collection at the designated pickup counter. He collects his food without queuing and makes it back to his next class on time.
