### Agenda & Topics Discussed
1.  **Project selection finalized** 
     We had 2 ideas for the project, the first one was creating a student card system for tracking students attends in lectures which can make the 80% attendance rule for qualifying for a DP dfully implemented and intergrated. 
     This could also be used to track the time the staff clocked in to the university, and if someone experiences a problem that needs confirmation that they were at school we could check if they were really in the schools premises.
     The 2nd project was creating an app for the school's cafeteria to help mitigate the long lines and have a formal application to update about the available menu of the day rather than having to know only when you are there that something is not available.This was to also help lecturers to be not integrated that much with students as they can order and pickup at the times they find suitable.
     3rd idea was creating an application that can analyse your matric results and tell you what courses you qualify for in SA and where to apply for the but we later realised that is an issue that has long been solved.

     After much consideration and evaluation, we discovered that even though the student card system is a great idea that could capture a lot of eye and make a great impat, it unfortunately also needs a heavy hardware system for scanning and also needs us to have the students and staff database from the school.
     The 2nd Cafeteria idea was the one we decided to go with, it also solves an issue faced by Walter Sisuslu University students and helps integrate a well informed and organized application seamlessly to help wioth functionality.
