#Project name: UniBites
A web-based pre-ordering system for campus cafeterias that allows students to order food in advance and select a pickup time, while giving cafeteria staff real-time demand visibility to reduce waste and improve preparation efficiency.

##Problem Statement
Campus cafeterias currently face two interconnected problems that negatively affect both students and staff every day.

Student problem
During peak lunch hours (12:00–13:00), students face long, unpredictable queues at the cafeteria. There is no way to order in advance, no visibility into what food is available before arriving, and no way to plan a reliable lunch break around a specific wait time. This wastes valuable student time and creates a frustrating daily experience.

Cafeteria staff problem
Staff have no advance visibility into how many students will order, what they will order, or when they will arrive. Food preparation is based on rough estimates and past experience. This leads to two costly outcomes: over-preparation results in significant food wastage at the end of each day, while under-preparation during peak times causes shortages, longer queues, and dissatisfied students.

Root cause
The absence of a pre-ordering system means both sides are operating blind. Students have no way to signal demand in advance, and staff have no data to act on. This app directly addresses that information gap.

##Target Users
1. Campus students who eat at the cafeteria daily.
2. Cafeteria cooks, servers, and managers.
